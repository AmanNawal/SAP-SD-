\## Plant \& Shipping point \& Storage location determinations





\### Plant determination



* Plant will determine in sales order based on priority below



&nbsp;	1. CMIR (Customer Material Info Record)

&nbsp;	2. Customer Master

&nbsp;	3. Material Master



\### Shipping point determination (OVL2)

&nbsp;	

* Shipping point is determined in sales order based on the following combination.



&nbsp;	1. Shipping Condition (From customer master shipping tab)

&nbsp;	2. Loading Group (From material master sales and general tab)

&nbsp;	3. Plant (will take from sales order)



\### Storage Location determination (OVL3)



&nbsp;	1. Storage condition (From material master - plant storage tab 1)

&nbsp;	2. Shipping point (Shipping point from sales order)

&nbsp;	3. Plant (plant from sales order)
